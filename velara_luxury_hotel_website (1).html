Our Story

A Legacy of Timeless Hospitality

Nestled on the shores of Lake Pichola, Velara was conceived in 1932 by Maharaja Sajjan Singh as a private retreat for royalty and distinguished guests. Today it stands as one of India's most celebrated luxury destinations.

Every detail — from hand-laid marble courtyards to bespoke silverware — honours the Maharaja's vision that a guest should feel not merely accommodated, but transported to another world.
